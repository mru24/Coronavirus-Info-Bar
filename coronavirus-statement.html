Coronavirus Statement

Mr Muck's Garden Supplies will continue to service both existing and new customers until circumstances dictate otherwise. We have a range of quality manures and composts in stock for delivery throughout the UK.

The health of customers, particularly the old and vulnerable is of upmost importance. By wearing surgical gloves, regular hand washing between deliveries and social distancing, we are working hard to maintain a high level of health and hygiene.

If you have questions, need advice or if we can help in any way - do not hesitate to get in touch, call 01302 358158.
